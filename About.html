About US




At Children of Mother Earth and Social Welfare Society, we conduct regular food drives to support needy and hungry individuals in government hospitals and other locations. Through the generous donations of our supporters and the dedication of our volunteers, we provide nutritious meals to those facing food insecurity, offering them hope and support in difficult times. Join us in our mission to fight hunger and make a difference in the lives of those who need it most.
